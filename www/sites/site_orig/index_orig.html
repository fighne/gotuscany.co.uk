Villa Pagiano di Sotto

 

The Villa Pagiano di Sotto with it's two adjoining apartments are set in an idyllic setting of peace and tranquility with magnificent views of the sea and surrounding hills. This region of Marema has stunning sandy beaches with crystal clear waters that are within half an hour's drive and are blue flag rated. Within a short distance of the Villa are the local villages Montemassi, Sassfortino and Roccatederighi. The perfect location for just relaxing and exploring at your leisure ....nearby unspoilt beaches, medieval villages, Siena 45 min's, Florence 90 min's, and even Rome is within two hours drive. There is plenty to do and see for both adults and children (please see page "region" for more information). Pisa is the best airport to fly to with Easyjet, Ryanair or BA offering car hire and is a 90 minute drive.

Whether you are a couple renting one apartment or a group renting the entire Villa, this is an ideal holiday location that offers the perfect balance of a traditional Tuscany home with the comforts that you appreciate. Most importantly it is realistically priced so that you will be tempted to return again and again......
