Apartment 2

This first floor apartment is reached by it's own private staircase. It has 1 double bedroom with a higher mezzanine level sleeping one adult or child. There is a fully equipped integral kitchen with hob, fridge/freezer, iron & ironing board and small eating area. The lounge area has a large and comfortable double sofa bed, satellite TV and CD player/radio. There is a shower room with a WC and bidet. Bedrooms and bathrooms have linen, towels and hairdryer provided.

This apartment has its own balcony area (see photo) and outside there is a shaded patio with wonderful views over the 5 acres of olive groves and vineyards with the Mediterranean sea in the distance. There is a table with chairs and a shared BBQ. There is ample parking and access to this property is down a gravel track of about a mile or so from the main road.
