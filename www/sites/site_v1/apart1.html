Apartment 1

This ground floor apartment has it's own private entrance with 1 twin bedroom, and a double bedroom (1 double bed and one single) with french doors out onto a patio area with superb views of the Med. There is a fully equipped integral kitchen with hob, fridge/freezer, washing machine, iron & ironing board. The lounge area has a wood burning stove, sofa, satellite TV and CD player/radio. There is a bathroom with a large shower and WC. Bedrooms and bathrooms have linen, towels and hairdryer provided.

Outside there is a private, shaded patio with wonderful views over the 5 acres of olive groves and vineyards with the Mediterranean sea in the distance. There is a table with chairs and a shared BBQ. There is ample parking and access to this property is down a gravel track of about a mile or so from the main road.
