Go Tuscany..

Privately owned traditional family run villa with adjoining apartments in Tuscany, surrounded by olive groves and vineyards with magnificent views down to the Mediterranean sea. Villa Pagiano offers a selection of accommodation for 2/14 guests. The villa itself can be rented as a whole or for smaller groups each apartment sleep 2/5.

Beautiful Villa Pagiano is situated in an idyllic unspoilt part of Tuscany called Maremma. Well known for it's stunning coastline, archaeological sites and wildlife, it is an ideal base for touring Tuscany. Pisa and Rome airports are under 2 hrs drive. The villa is available between March and September and continues to offer good value affordable family holiday accommodation.
