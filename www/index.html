Holiday Villa & Apartments with Communal Above Ground Swimming Pool in Tuscany

Our beautiful, relaxed and informally family run Villa and Apartments are situated in a picturesque and unspoilt region in Southern Tuscany called Maremma near Grosseto. This area is well known for it's stunning coastline, archaeological sites and wildlife, an ideal base for holidaying and touring in Tuscany and Lazio.

  • Idyllic, peaceful and secluded setting with stunning views down to the sea
  • Individual shaded patios for both apartments and villa.
  • With above ground 9m x 5m communal swimming pool.
  • Traditionally restored in keeping with Tuscan colours using only natural materials.
  • Beautiful clean sandy beaches within 25 minutes drive.
  • Superb medieval villages with trattorias, restaurants, shops, festas, cafes and amenities (3kms).
  • Visit Pisa, Florence, Rome, Siena and Island of Elba and Giglio, all within easy reach.
  • Plenty of activities for families including Aqua park in Follonica, local waterfalls and thermal baths.
  • Fly with Easyjet Ryanair and BA to Pisa or Rome. Car hire is essential.

The Villa and Apartments are available from May to September and continue to offer good value, affordable holiday accommodation in the very heart of Tuscany. Surrounded by lush olive groves and vineyards with magnificent views right down to the Mediterranean sea. The Villa and Apartments offer a selection of accommodation for 2/14 guests. They can be rented either as a whole or for smaller groups each apartment sleep 2/5. Please confirm with owner that the swimming pool will be open, occasionally due to very hot summers there can be water shortage for pools.
