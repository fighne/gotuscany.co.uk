Holiday Villa & Apartments in unspoilt Tuscany

Beautiful Pagiano di Soto is situated in an idyllic unspoilt part of Tuscany called Maremma. Well known for it's stunning coastline, archaeological sites and wildlife, it is an ideal base for touring Tuscany. Pisa and Rome airports are under 2 hrs drive.

  • Idyllic peaceful and secluded setting with stunning views down to the sea.
  • Traditionally restored in keeping with Tuscan colours using only natural, eco friendly local materials.
  • Beautiful clean sandy beaches within 25 minutes drive.
  • Two local swimming pools nearby.
  • Individual shaded patios for both apartments and villa.
  • Superb medieval villages with trattorias, restaurants, shops, cafes and amenities within 5 mins.
  • Visit Pisa, Florence, Rome, Sienna and Elba.
  • Plenty of activities for families including Aqua park in Follonica, local waterfalls and thermal baths.
  • Fly with Easyjet Ryanair and BA. Car hire is essential.

The villa and apartments are available from March to September and continue to offer good value affordable holiday accommodation. Pagiano di Soto is surrounded by olive groves and vineyards with magnificent views down to the Mediterranean sea. The villa and apartments offer a selection of accommodation for 2/14 guests. Pagiano di Soto can be rented as a whole or for smaller groups each apartment sleep 2/5.
